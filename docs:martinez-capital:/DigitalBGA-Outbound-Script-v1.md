## INTRODUCTION

## Hello <First Name>?

Hi, this is JUST <Agent First Name> getting back to you. I noticed you recently inquired about POSSIBLY looking at some final expense coverage to protect your family. I’m reaching out to see if I can help. (Expect a “Smokescreen Objection”)

Were you looking for something in particular…or were you just wanting to go through the different options available to you?

What was it that prompted you to fill out our form and request information about covering your final expenses?

Great. Now is this coverage just for yourself or for you AND a loved one?

Here’s what we’ll do over the next few minutes. I need to:

(Continue with Inbound Script)

## SMOKESCREEN RESPONSES

I’m not interested: “Oh I understand. Are you not interested because you can’t afford it or because you think you might not qualify?” (They may say they already purchased, then go to that response.)

• If can’t afford: “Well we do have plans starting as low as \$25/mo. Would that be more affordable for you?”

• If can’t qualify: “Well we have plans that don’t have any health questions where everyone qualifies. Would it be appropriate to see if you’re eligible for those?”

I already purchased: “Awesome. When did you get that taken care of?” Follow up with: “While I have you on the phone, would it be reasonable to offer you another quote to make sure you got the best policy available?"

I don’t have any money: “Oh, you don’t have to set anything up today. I’m just here to get you the information and it’s up to you what you decide to do with it. Would that be appropriate?”

I didn’t request anything / I don’t remember: “I apologize. This may be on me for taking a while to get back to you. This is about protecting your family from out of pocket funeral costs and final expenses. Does that ring a bell?”

I don’t have time: “I understand. Would it be ok if I took 30 seconds to share this information and after if it doesn’t make sense, we can hang up. I don’t want to keep calling if it’s nothing you’re interested in. Sound fair?” (Then go over what you’ll do on the call to disarm them.)

• “Great. My job is to JUST get you this information so I’ll show you how these state-approved final expense plans work, determine what you may qualify for, go over pricing from the carriers… and then YOU decide if this is something you would like to move forward with. Sound fair enough?” (Then continue with script. If they stop you, then set a firm appointment since they know what to expect on the call.)

If lead comes off as rude or angry: “I’m sorry, I didn’t mean to offend you.   
This is JUST <Agent First Name>”, then proceed to script.
